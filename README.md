Taking inspiration from Embark Studios' acclaimed **ARC Raiders**, our health system focuses on **visual feedback over numbers**. Instead of cluttered stat displays, the HUD communicates your status through intuitive, animated bars that feel alive.

### How It Works

**Segmented Shield & Health Bars**

- 5-sector segmented design for clear visual chunking
- Shield bar (top) absorbs damage before health
- Health bar (bottom) shows your vital status
- Each segment represents 20% â€” know your status at a glance

**Dynamic Damage Feedback**

- **Damage trailing** â€” When hit, a red "ghost bar" lingers showing damage taken, then smoothly catches down to your actual health
- **Screen shake** â€” Subtle shake effect on damage for visceral feedback
- **Color flash** â€” Bars and borders flash red momentarily on impact
- No floating numbers or damage text â€” just clean visual communication

**Healing & Shield Regeneration**

- **Animated fill stripes** â€” Green diagonal stripes animate across health when healing
- **Orange stripes** for shield regeneration
- **Step-based animation** â€” Health fills in satisfying increments, not instant jumps
- Visual feedback that feels rewarding without being distracting
